Network Visualization Tutorial

1. Visualization technique description The visualization technique that I am going to demonstrate in this notebook is network visualization. First, I wanted to cover a couple of concepts that I am using in this notebook. Network comprises of edges and nodes. Nodes are separate entities, for example, people in a social network. Edges are the connection points between the nodes, for example, relationship between people in a social network. Visual representation of edges and nodes is a network visualization.
